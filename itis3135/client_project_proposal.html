Client Project Proposal

This overview is for a project that is being worked on by both Jacob Kilcrease and Noah Harden. We have gotten approval from Professor D.I. von Briesen to collaborate on this project.

Description and Purpose

In collaboration with Noah Harden we will be working together to redo the fabclt website. This website is for the Charlotte, NC crowd for the trading card game “Flesh and Blood”. This site will allow people to see events that are happening in the area from local weeklies to major tournaments. People can view what events have happened and look at the winners for major events and see what decks they won with at said events.

Intended Users

This website is for Flesh and Blood players in the Charlotte area.

Overview of website content:

  1. Overview of the Flesh and Blood scene in Charlotte, NC
  2. Pictures of people and event in Charlotte
  3. Calendar for events
  4. Event RSVP
  5. Event winners
  6. Winners decklists
  7. How to play guide
  8. Further Information
  9. Community Contact

Wireframe

Site Map

Page Design

  • Home- Landing page for the website. Has general information about the FABCLT Community
  • Locations- Listing of local stores that support Flesh and Blood with an integrated map to see the locations of each store
  • Events- Listing of local Flesh and Blood Events with an calendar to see when events are
  • Seasons winners- Shows who has won the competitive events in the area and a link to their decklist for said event
  • Join us- page with connections to people in the area and the community discord server
  • People- List of players and judges in the area with pictures of each
  • Learn to play- lists guides and videos on how to play Flesh and Blood along with recommendation for new players

Dynamic Functions

  • Locations - map
  • Events- calendar, event RSVP, map
  • Seasons winners- Decklist display and card database
